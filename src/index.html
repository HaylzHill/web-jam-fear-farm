FAQs

What ages can attend the Haunted House?

Both the Haunted House and Corn Maze feature full effect with animatronics and a full acting staff. Both attractions are designed to scare even the most seasoned haunt fan. We do not recommended our attractions for children under 8 years old. Children 8 – 12 years old must be accompanied by an adult.

Can I take pictures or videos inside the attractions?

Photography/video of any kind is not permitted inside the attractions.

Is there onsite security?

We have a full security staff on hand at all times who have undergone training. All security staff are in communication via radios and we have infrared cameras placed throughout so we can see you even when you can’t see us.
