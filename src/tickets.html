Tickets

Come experience the Haunt House and Corn Maze. Each night features our world class actors, top of the line props and many surprises.

Fear Farm is open from 6pm to 12pm Fridays & Saturdays, Sundays. Attraction is open weather and field conditions permitting.

All tickets are time slotted in 1 hour increments. Please arrive onsite & enter the queue line during your time slot. Reserve your slot online to guarantee your reservation. Payment will be taken on admission.

Pricing

Friday admission: $10

Weekend admission: $15
